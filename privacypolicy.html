StreaKO Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-24

StreaKO is a habit and streak tracker. This policy explains what data the app processes and why.

What the app stores on your device

StreaKO stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Habits you create, including name, optional description, emoji, color, and schedule settings.
  • Habit progress data, including streaks and completion history.
  • App preferences, including theme, dark mode, and language.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules notifications on your device. You can disable reminders at any time.

Health Connect

If you enable automatic tracking features, StreaKO can connect to Health Connect to read health data on your device. This is optional and disabled by default.

Data types we may access, only if you enable the feature and grant permission:

  • Steps: we read your total steps for today, from the start of the day until now, to optionally auto-complete a linked walking habit when you reach your step goal.
  • Sleep sessions: we read your sleep session durations for last night, a “night window,” to optionally auto-complete a linked sleep habit when you reach your sleep goal.

How we use this data:

  • We use steps and sleep data only to evaluate whether you met a goal and to mark a linked habit as completed.
  • We do not sell health data. We do not share health data with advertisers.
  • Health data is processed on-device. StreaKO does not upload your Health Connect data to our servers.

Advertising

The app shows ads using Google AdMob. Depending on your region and choices, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

  • AdMob may process device information such as the advertising ID and IP address to deliver and measure ads.
  • In regions where consent is required, for example EEA/UK, the app uses Google's consent flow, UMP, before requesting ads.

Analytics

StreaKO does not include an analytics SDK in this build. If analytics are added in future versions, this policy will be updated.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. AdMob may process data for advertising purposes as described above.

Data export

You can export your habits to a CSV file stored in the app's documents directory on your device.

Data retention and deletion

StreaKO stores your habit data locally on your device and retains it until you delete it.

You can delete your data at any time:

  • In the app: Settings → Delete all data removes habits, completions, notes, and rewards stored by the app.
  • Uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data from your device.
  • Health Connect permissions can be removed at any time from Android / Health Connect settings.
  • You can also disable Steps Sync / Sleep Tracking inside the app.
