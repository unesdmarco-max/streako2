# StreaKO Privacy Policy Last updated: 2026-04-19 StreaKO is a habit and streak tracker. This policy explains what data the app processes and why. ## What the app stores on your device StreaKO stores the following data locally on your device: - Habits you create (name, optional description, emoji, color, schedule settings). - Habit progress data (streaks and completion history). - App preferences (theme, dark mode, language). ## Notifications If you enable reminders, the app schedules notifications on your device. You can disable reminders at any time. ## Advertising (Google AdMob) The app shows ads using Google AdMob. Depending on your region and choices, ads may be personalized or non-personalized. - AdMob may process device information such as the advertising ID and IP address to deliver and measure ads. - In regions where consent is required (for example EEA/UK), the app uses Google's consent flow (UMP) before requesting ads. ## Analytics StreaKO does not include an analytics SDK in this build. If analytics are added in future versions, this policy will be updated. ## Data sharing We do not sell your personal data. AdMob may process data for advertising purposes as described above. ## Data export You can export your habits to a CSV file stored in the app's documents directory on your device. ## Contact Privacy questions: `damrco00000@gmail.com`.
