StreaKO Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-28

StreaKO is a habit and streak tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how it is used, and what choices you have.

1. Information stored on your device

StreaKO primarily stores your habit data locally on your device.

  • Habits you create, including name, optional description, emoji, color, and schedule settings.
  • Habit progress, including streaks, completions, and notes.
  • App preferences such as language, theme, and reminder settings.
  • Reward and unlock status used by in-app features.

2. Notifications

If you enable reminders, StreaKO uses notification permission to send local habit reminders on your device. You can disable notifications at any time in Android settings or inside the app.

3. Optional Usage Access

StreaKO may offer optional automatic habit completion based on phone usage limits. If you choose to enable this feature, the app may request Android Usage Access.

When enabled, StreaKO may read:

  • Your daily screen-on count.
  • Your daily screen time total.

This data is used only to:

  • Check whether you stayed within the limits you configured.
  • Optionally auto-complete the linked habit on your device.

This usage data is processed on-device and is not uploaded to StreaKO servers. You can revoke Usage Access at any time from Android settings.

4. Advertising

StreaKO displays ads using Google AdMob.

The app may show these ad formats:

  • Banner ads.
  • Interstitial ads.
  • App open ads.
  • Native ads.
  • Rewarded ads and rewarded interstitial ads.

Google and its partners may process certain device and advertising-related data to deliver, personalize, measure, and protect ads, depending on your region and choices. This may include data such as advertising identifiers, IP address, device information, and ad interaction information.

5. Consent and privacy choices

In regions where consent is required, StreaKO uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to collect and manage ad consent before initializing ads.

If available in your region, you can review or update your ad privacy choices from the in-app "Ad choices" option.

6. Analytics

StreaKO does not include a separate analytics SDK in this build. If analytics are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

7. Backups and exports

StreaKO may let you create local exports or backups on your device, such as:

  • CSV exports of your habits.
  • JSON backups used for local restore/import.

These files are created on your device and remain under your control unless you choose to move or share them yourself.

8. Data sharing

StreaKO does not sell your personal data. Habit data and optional Usage Access data are not shared with StreaKO servers.

Ad-related data may be processed by Google AdMob and its partners as part of ad delivery and consent handling.

9. Data retention and deletion

Your habit data remains on your device until you remove it. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Using Settings > Delete all data inside the app.
  • Uninstalling the app from your device.

10. Children's privacy

StreaKO is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to consent to personal data processing on their own. If you believe data was provided improperly, please contact us.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
